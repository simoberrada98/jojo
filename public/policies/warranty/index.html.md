# Mining Hardware Warranty
> Coverage begins on the delivery date and assumes operation within documented environmental tolerances.

## Coverage Matrix
| Item | Coverage | Standard response |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Factory defects (boards, PSU, control board) | Covered for 24 months | Vendor-sourced replacement or repair; advance RMA in 3 business days once diagnostics are supplied |
| Dead on arrival hardware | Full replacement or refund within 14 days | Replacement unit shipped within 48 hours of approval |
| Firmware or software faults | Covered on approved firmware versions | Patch or rollback guidance within 1 business day |
| User-induced damage (liquid, overclock beyond spec) | Not covered | Damage report supplied within 5 business days |

## Service Windows & Turnaround
- Critical mining downtime: 24 hour initial response
- Partial degradation (single board offline): 3 business days
- Fleet issues impacting >25% units: executive call within 1 business day

| Stage | Owner | Action |

- Serial number and proof of purchase
- Diagnostics (event logs, photos)
- Environment details (power, cooling, firmware)
