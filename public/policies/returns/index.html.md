# Returns & Refunds

> Request an RMA before shipping hardware. Returns without an authorized RMA will be refused.

## Return Workflow

| Item                                    | Eligibility window    | Notes                                                                  |
| --------------------------------------- | --------------------- | ---------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Unused miners in original packaging     | 7 days from delivery  | 10% restocking fee to cover import and handling                        |
| Operational miners (powered and tested) | 14 days from delivery | Requires diagnostic logs and proof of issue; shipping paid by customer |
| Accessories, cables, power distribution | 30 days from delivery | Must be unopened to qualify for full refund                            |

## Exceptions & Inspection Notes

- Missing serial stickers or tamper seals are ineligible
- Custom firmware must be reverted to stock prior to inspection
- Consumables (thermal paste, filters) are non-returnable once opened
- Hosting customers follow onsite swap programme per hosting SLA

## Refund Timeline

| Step              | Timeline                           | Action taken                                                                 |
| ----------------- | ---------------------------------- | ---------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Return requested  | Within 1 business day              | RMA number and prepaid label (if eligible)                                   |
| Hardware received | 2-3 business days                  | Inspection and hashboard testing                                             |
| Refund issued     | 3-5 business days after inspection | Funds to original payment; crypto refunds at USD spot rate at time of return |

## Start a Return

Email [returns@jhuangnyc.com](mailto:returns@jhuangnyc.com?subject=Return%20request) with order number, serials, photos, and issue description.
